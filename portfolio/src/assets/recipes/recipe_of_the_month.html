Recipe of the Month:

Cheesecake

  • 200 Gramm Lotus Cookies (for the cake base)
  • 80 Grams melted butter
  • 800 Gram "Philadelphia" Cream Cheese
  • 150 Grams of Sugar
  • 1 Lemon
  • 4 Eggs
  • 180ml heavy cream
  • 1 package of vanilla pudding
  • 250 Gram Sour cream
  • 30 Gram Powder Sugar
  1. Put the Lotus Cookies in a freezing bag, crumble the cookies to fine dust and mix with the melted butter.
  2. Now we put our cake base mass in a springform pan greased around the edges and press the cake base mass firmly to the bottom until it levels out
  3. Now we mix the Cream Cheese with our sugar and the juice of the lemon. Stir this mix until it's smooth
  4. Into this mix, we add the eggs one by one.
  5. Put the heavy cream and the vanilla pudding powder in a bowl and stir them until they are smooth as well and mix with our Cream Cheese mass.
  6. Mix the Sour Creme with the Powder Sugar and put it into the mix aswell.
  7. Now we put our mix in the springform pan and even it out
  8. Preheat the oven at 180 degrees two-sided heat.
  9. Put the cake inside the oven for ~45 minutes.
  10. Enjoy! 🍰
